## Full Description

Yaniv is a fast-paced card game where timing, memory, and smart decisions matter.

Play online with friends, create private rooms, invite players, add AI opponents, chat during the game, and enjoy smooth card animations with tropical visuals and sound effects.

Features:

- Online rooms with friends
- Private room codes
- AI players
- In-game chat
- Sound effects and background music
- Yaniv and Assaf round events
- Score tracking
- Hebrew and English support

Whether you already know Yaniv or are learning the game, this app gives you a simple and fun way to play from your phone.
