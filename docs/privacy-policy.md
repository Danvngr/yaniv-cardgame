# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Yaniv ("the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how you can contact us about your data.

## Information We Collect

The app may collect the following information:

- Account information, such as a user ID, username, avatar, and email address when you sign in.
- Game information, such as scores, game history, player statistics, room information, and match activity.
- Friend and invitation information needed to support online play.
- Chat messages sent inside a game room.
- Basic technical information required to operate the app and keep the service stable.

The app does not collect precise location, payment information, health information, photos, videos, or phone contacts.

## How We Use Information

We use the information to:

- Create and manage user accounts.
- Let players create, join, and play in online game rooms.
- Support multiplayer gameplay, friends, invitations, and in-game chat.
- Show scores, game history, and player statistics.
- Improve app stability, security, and user experience.

## Data Storage and Service Providers

The app uses Firebase services to store account and game-related data. The app also uses a game server for real-time multiplayer communication.

These services process data only as needed to operate the app and its online features.

## Chat Messages

Messages sent in the in-game chat may be visible to other players in the same game room. Please do not share personal, private, or sensitive information in chat.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with advertisers. Data may be processed by service providers that help operate the app, such as Firebase and the multiplayer server.

## Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user data, including authenticated access and Firebase security rules where applicable. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

## Data Retention and Deletion

We keep data for as long as needed to provide the app's features, maintain game history, and operate the service.
